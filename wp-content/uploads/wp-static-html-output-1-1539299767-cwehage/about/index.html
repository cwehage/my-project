Elite Artisans is a decorative painting and faux finishing company serving the Baltimore-Washington-Virginia corridor with impeccable craftsmanship and detail. Whether it’s walls, ceilings, furniture, fixtures, woodwork or trim, Elite Artisans will transform your home or business with custom colors, elegant finishes and artistic flair.

 

Founded in 2004, Elite Artisans’ dedicated artists personally handle your project from basecoat to completion. We’ve worked with some of the top designers in the Baltimore area on commercial and residential projects — and have traveled up and down the East Coast to accommodate clients in New England, New York, and St. Thomas, USVI.

 

With over 15 years experience, Founder Lynn Fuller has immense knowledge of the craft and works with homeowners, designers, architects and general contractors to find the right finish for your home or business. Elite Artisans is highly trained to execute beautiful finishes, with a specialty in woodgrain (faux bois) and matching existing woodwork or finishes.
